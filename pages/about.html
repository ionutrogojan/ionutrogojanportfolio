Hey, what's up? I'm Ionut Rogojan but you can call me John, it will make your life easier (which is my main goal)
I call myself a graphic design but I'm still quite fresh in the industry. I use my free time to improve my skills in illustration, logo design, photo manipulation, 3d, video editing and production, visual game assets and pixel art. So far I have worked with different companies and individuals from various industries, such as Hospitality, Mass Production, Home Decor and Music.
I grew up on cartoon series aired on Disney Channel and Cartoon-Network and I use that to reference all of my designs with a hint of modern trending styles.
Also, I'm a perfectionist and unofficially OCD diagnosed. I like to take my time to make sure everything lines up perfectly and all the values are a perfect .00 or an even number, at least.
